Dumpling Dex — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 May 2026

Short version: we don’t collect your personal data.

Dumpling Dex is a collector companion app for mystery “squishy dumpling” toys. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your information. It is written to be honest and easy to read.

What we collect

Nothing personal. Dumpling Dex does not require an account, a login, an email address, or any sign-up. We do not ask for your name, contacts, location, photos, or any personally identifying information.

Where your data lives

Everything you create in the app — the dumplings you mark as owned, your pull log, your watchlist, your sightings, and your settings — is stored only on your device. It is not uploaded to us or anyone else. If you delete the app, that data is removed with it.

Anonymous community stats (optional)

A future version of Dumpling Dex may let you contribute anonymous pull statistics so the app can show what the community is finding. If and when that feature is enabled:

  • It is optional and can be turned off at any time in Settings → Community stats.
  • It would send only a random, per-install identifier (a UUID that is not linked to your identity), the dumpling/series involved, and a timestamp.
  • It would never include your name, email, device IDs, or any personal information, and it is not used for tracking or advertising.

In the current version, behavior is local-only — nothing leaves your device.

No ads, no tracking, no selling data

Dumpling Dex contains no advertising, no third-party analytics or tracking SDKs, and no in-app purchases. We do not sell or share any data, because we don’t collect any.

Children’s privacy

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no personal information from anyone, including children. Because it is account-free and anonymous by design, it does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

Not affiliated with RMS USA

Dumpling Dex is an independent, fan-made tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or sponsored by RMS USA or any retailer. Product and series names are used for identification only.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes (for example, when the optional community feature launches), we will update this page and the “Last updated” date above.
